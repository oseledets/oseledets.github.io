Time evolution and optimization with MPS

21 August 2014

The projector-splitting scheme in the TT/MPS format is a natural way to computing dynamics of quantum spin systems Hamiltonians with long-range interaction. We have proposed a unified approach that leads to a cheap and efficient computational scheme that can be applied to any Hamiltonian, represented in the MPO/TT form.
