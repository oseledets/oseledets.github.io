Alexander Mikhalev

PhD Student at CMC MSU, research engineer at Skoltech

Research interests

Alexander is the main developer of the h2tools package for the black-box approximation of large dense matrices by sampling. He is also an author of the new algorithm for the rectangular maximum-volume submatrices, see https://bitbucket.org/muxas/rect_maxvol.
